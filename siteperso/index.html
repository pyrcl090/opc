How experiences influence designs is an interview style zine introducing how my friend and colleague Izzy Wu's lived experiences have influenced her designs.

A collection of Typographic and Graphic designs focused on critiqueing overconsumption and structural systems in the design community.

nouille

My stable diet consists of mainly nouilles so I am making them into a typeface so that I can consume them visually as well. This typeface embodies what it means to be a nouille.

Speaking With the Frame

Speaking with the Frame is an interactive website useing the A.P.I database created by my friend and colleague Moxin Xu. The website allows you to click on frames to see them speak to you.

Broken Nature

For my Flexible formats project, I created an identity for the Broken Nature exhibit @ the MoMA which featured the work of enviromental designers and engineers.
