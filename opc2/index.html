Anaïs’ top 5 Favorite Artists + Designers
1
DO HO SUH

I fell upon the work of Do Ho Suh in secondary school and have been in love with it ever since. Each one of his pieces looks so ethereal but at the same time homey and beautiful.
2
YUN SOHYUN

Yun Sohyun’s furniture is always spectacular to me, the fun colors and the ability to customise within her designs is genius.
3
SAM BAILEY

Sam Bailey is a coder/graphic designer that uses code to manipulate images in a 3-d space to create some of the most beautiful warped images.
4
AMOAKO BOAFO

Amoako Boafo is an amazing fine artist that creates portraits useing his fingers to paint, creating intimate portraits.
5
HAGIHARA TAKUYA

Hagihara Takuya, is a graphic artist who uses gradients and patterns to create beautiful images. I feel like entering his designs whenever I see one.
